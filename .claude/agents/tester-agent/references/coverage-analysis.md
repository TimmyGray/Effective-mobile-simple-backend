# Analyze Coverage

## Outcome

Deliver a concise coverage assessment highlighting critical untested paths and priority additions.

## Context

Use `docs/QUALITY_SCORE.md` and `docs/exec-plans/tech-debt-tracker.md` to align recommendations
with known risk areas and roadmap priorities.

## Scope

In scope: risk-based test gap analysis and actionable next-test recommendations.
Out of scope: broad architecture recommendations not connected to testability.
