# Review Architecture

## Outcome

Provide an architecture review that flags structural risks, boundary violations, and design drift.

## Context

Evaluate against `docs/design-docs/core-beliefs.md`, `docs/CONVENTIONS.md`, and `docs/SECURITY.md`.
Critical checks include centralized authz, testability, and future extensibility of role/permission models.

## Scope

In scope: architecture-level correctness and maintainability findings.
Out of scope: pure style-only feedback.
